Properties:

Allow us to interface with the shader and to change values.
Updating the property of the material, changes the corresponding value of the shader.

We also need to define our variables within our sader to shader acess them.
The uniform keyword is used to define initial values, but not required.

Types of properties:
2D - Textures, normal maps, etc
Color - Simple colors
Range(0.0, 1.0) - Sliders from 0.0 to 1.0
float - Floating points.
Vector - Vector3 position
Cube - Cube maping.
